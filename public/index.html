Why Precision Learning?

Organisational learning is an essential element for the survival of any organisation in this fast paced technological and business landscape.

Vocational, technology and IT knowledge deprecates much faster, with their half life reducing rapidly. Upskilling and obsolescence management on a continuous basis without compromising on productive employee time is a major challenge for Enterprises

Navigated Learning

A form of “Precision Learning” environment that identifies learning needs at high precision and curates specific learning experiences. It addresses three independent learning concerns: Scale,Individualization, Social elements. It is based on two underlying concepts:

Social Spaces

Stories

Create Logical spaces representing competencies

Embed learning resources, organizational elements, project requirements and any relevant entity into the competency spaces.

Learning pathways and topical neighborhoods representing coherent learning experiences

Curate personalized learning routes based on learner proficiency and activity.

Mediate between learners to facilitate narrative diversity and richer insights
